Puzzled

Play

Your aim is to complete the puzzle in the solution square in the middle of the window.

Move by dragging pieces (left mouse button).

You can rotate a piece by clicking the right mouse button.

You can flip a piece by clicking the right mouse button while holding the shift-key.

Levels

  • Level one is the easiest. It displays the pieces in the orientation they have in the solution.
  • Level two is the moderate. It displays some pieces rotated from the orientation they have in the solution.
  • Level three is the hard. It displays some pieces flipped as well as rotated from the orientation they have in the solution.

Puzzles

The puzzles themselves vary in difficulty.

In addition to the original puzzle, there are a number of other puzzles provided (as .dat files that you can load.)

You can also generate a new puzzle using the Puzzle Generate command

You can create your own puzzles. Puzzles are just text files with a .dat extension with 8 lines of comma separated numbers (1 to 8) representing colors.

History

A history of results is stored in the PuzzleHistory.txt file.
